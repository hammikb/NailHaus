Shipping Policy

NailHaus is a marketplace. Each vendor ships their own items and provides tracking when available.

Processing times

Most vendors ship within 1–5 business days depending on whether sets are made-to-order. You’ll see tracking in your account once a label is created.

Tracking

When a vendor purchases a shipping label through NailHaus, we automatically attach the tracking number to the shipment record.

Shipping issues

If a package appears lost or delivered but not received, contact the vendor first. If you can’t resolve it, submit a report and our team will review.

Returns & Refunds

Policies vary by vendor. We encourage fair, clear resolutions for buyers and vendors.

Hygiene & custom items

Because press-on nails are personal-use items, many vendors cannot accept returns once opened. Custom sizing requests may also be non-returnable.

Damaged or incorrect orders

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact the vendor within 48 hours with photos. Most issues can be solved with a replacement or partial refund.

Need help?

If you’re unable to resolve an issue with the vendor, submit a report and our team will review.
